A TRIP TO ENGLAND

In my recent visit to England, I visited a few cities and had so much fun that i never expected. Firstly, I visited the city of Manchester where I met so many old friends who gave me a warm welcome and made me feel at home. Furthermore, I traveled to the city of London after spending 3 days in Manchester with my friends. London is so beautiful but over crowdy, on like Manchester with less population of people. After spending a week in England, I had the best time of my life but unfortunately I have to travel back to Africa where I belong. However, the trip was overall worthwhile and I will love to visit again

Things to do in England

  1. Manchester Museum:


    The Manchester museum is one of the best that I have seen in a while compared to the lists of museum I have visited. It has one of the best view and interior designs you could imagine.


  2. A visit to the london bridge:


    London bridge is so wonderfully made and so pleasing to watch. It has been ond of my dream to see the london bridge as a child, the dream became a reality when i visited England. I love the work of intelligence done on the bridge.


  3. A visit to the park:


    There are so many parks in England where anyone could go and relax and have some family time or lonely time, there are lots of activities that one could do in the park as there are so many sporting facilities available.


My Overall Experience In England

My experience in England was really memorable, Proper English tea, red buses, black cabs, everything felt new. I wandered the British Museum for hours, completely absorbed. I visited a cozy pub and watched locals chat about football like it was religion. Lovely people and friendly culture. Worth every penny.
